Key Highlights

Market Opportunity

Sized the home hydroponics TAM at $4.5B globally with a 12% CAGR. Identified urban millennials in Tier-1 Indian cities and US metros as the primary SAM ‚Äî high disposable income, limited outdoor space, health-conscious.

User Personas

Built three distinct personas: the Health-Conscious Urban Professional (primary), the Sustainability Advocate, and the Home Chef ‚Äî each with distinct motivations, willingness to pay, and product expectations.

Product Strategy

Recommended a "hardware-led, software-monetized" model: low-margin hardware to acquire users, high-margin subscription for grow plans, seed kits, and community features.

GTM Approach

Phased GTM: D2C launch via Instagram/YouTube (lifestyle content), followed by Amazon marketplace, then retail partnerships with premium grocery chains (Whole Foods, Nature's Basket).
